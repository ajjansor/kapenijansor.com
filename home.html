Kape ni Jansor

Experience the delightful flavor of a pure organic coffee beans that can only be found in a small town in the Philippines.

The small town of Lavezares N. Samar is currently the biggest supplier of top quality coffee in the Philippines and is also famous around the world.

The coffee beans are planted with love by the local villagers. Harvested with care and processed in multiple steps to ensure the top quality of coffee will be served.

Anthony James T. Jansor

Anthony James T. Jansor, the owner of Kape ni Jansor was born on April 2, 2002, in Catarman Doctors Hospital and was the youngest child of Santiago C. Jansor and Ma. Amparo T. Jansor.

He honed his passion in planting coffee and continued it as his source of income even during his college years in University of Eastern Philippines.
